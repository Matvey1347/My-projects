About

Core Freedom Protections in Trump's 2024 Platform:

1

Protection of Free Speech - A commitment to defending the right to express opinions freely, especially against perceived government or media suppression.

2

Freedom of Religion - Support for religious liberty, ensuring that individuals and organizations can practice and express their beliefs without restriction.

3

Right to Bear Arms - A promise to uphold the Second Amendment, protecting Americans' rights to own and carry firearms as a safeguard of personal and national security.

4

Educational Freedom - An initiative aimed at keeping schools free from "radical ideologies" and upholding parental rights in children’s education, including protection against specific curricular content.
