Gunma, the adorable horse mascot of Gunma Prefecture, truly stands out among Japan’s many mascots. Rooted in the region’s history of horse breeding, his charm reflects the meaning behind Gunma’s kanji (群馬, "Group of Horses"). Whether riding buses, celebrating holidays, or donning a lab coat, Gunma-chan brings joy to locals at every turn.
